pARk

The public Augmented Reality kinectome is an immersive internet designed according to the ATOWN framework. pARk internet is peer-to-peer connecting users as long as they are near other connected devices. Unlike on the World Wide Web, where content is found on flat documents called websites, pARk content is viewed as an immersive overlay over relavant physical objects in an environment collectively called a Hyper Real Space.
