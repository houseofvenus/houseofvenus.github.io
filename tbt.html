Every Sunday evening we have a discussion on "My Sh0w" featuring a variety of random topics sourced from our weekly experiences and/or the news cycle. In light of the events of last week, our app has been overrun with one news story in particular. The ensuing conversation led us to change the app's name to "0ur Town" and participate in activities where we could support those affected by the ongoing crisis of police brutality in the United States. This led to us participating in an online town hall featuring the Los Angeles Police Commission and the city's current Chief of Police: Michel Moore. In light of the reflections made by the citizens of LA on Moore's career in addition to the disappointing public remarks he made recently, our panelists believe that he should step down as part of major reforms to policing in Los Angeles and across the United States.
